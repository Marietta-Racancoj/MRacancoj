Welcome to my personal page!

Bio

I grew up in Pennsylvania surrounded by mountains and nature. I am passionate about technology and love learning new programming languages. I graduated with a bachelor's in Music Education with a concentration in classical vocals and currently work as a choir director and principal organist for a church by the Jersey shore. I discovered my love of programming and the software development process later on, but am nevertheless extremely passionate about learning as much as I can in the field and building my knowledge of the field.

Hobbies

In my free time, I enjoy walking at my local reservoir, coding, attending festivals, and exploring New Jersey, Pennsylvania, and New York State for unique food with my family and two close friends. I enjoy reading, cooking, collecting Funko Pops, occasionally working construction jobs with my dad, and learning about different cultures and histories around the world. I am half Indigenous and over the Summer when my job is less hectic, I enjoy attending cultural festivals, especially Pow Wows in New Jersey and New York state to reconnect with my heritage. Though because of time constraints I have not been able to practice in a while, a personal hobby goal is to increase my pull strength in archery to a 50lb bow and eventually 75lb. I would also like to get better at traditional cooking, beadwork, and become fully fluent in Spanish and learn more of the K'iche Maya language to better connect with my heritage.
